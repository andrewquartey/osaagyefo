Who We Are

Osaagyefo is a product of Fresh Foods Fhana Ltd. At Fresh Foods our focus is on growing, marketing, distributing and processing agricultural products from Ghana.

The Product

We grow our own products and in some cases buy from local farmers. Our branded rice, Osaagyefo is enjoyed by many Ghanaians each day. Our rice is predominantly “jasmine” with varieties like “xbika” and “Saudi” being occasionally marketed.

The Vision

To have an African eat a variety of Osaagyefo product at least once a day
