Welcome to Osaagyefo

Osaagyefo is a product of fresh foods ghana Ltd. At Fresh Foods our focus is on growing, marketing, distributing and processing agricultural products from Ghana.

Our Story

Since our inception 4 years ago by a group of young professionals; we have steadily grown to deliver rice to various shops and markets in Accra and Kasoa.

At Osaagyefo we work closely with local farmers to help them improve the quality and standard of rice they deliver to the Ghanaian market. Our goal is to ensure that they are profitable, and we are also profitable enough to reinvest in the communities in which we operate.
